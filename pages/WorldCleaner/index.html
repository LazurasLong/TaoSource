What is World Cleaner?

World Cleaner aims to be a World Wide Social Network to motivate Individuals and Companies to change their own behavior.

How it works?

World Cleaner is a "Game". In this "Game", users must make a Video Stream as a proof of concept of their own actions. Their actions are related to different cathegories.

What Cathegories are you talking about?

We believe that the basic principles of nature care are: Nature it self ( plants, florest, rivers, sea), Animals ( all sort of ... )and lastly humans.

So, what kind of actions a user need to do in this cathegories?

It depends on what cathegory we are talking to.

If we take a Nature Care, users can treat plants, clean rivers, the sea or even the florest. The principle objective is to help nature to be less polluted, resulting is cleanest and secure place for everyone.

When helping animals, we need to give them what they diserve: freedom, food and a place to sleep. We need to respect them, not to kill them, or we are like them too.

Human care is more complex,( as it is the same ( mostly)) just because we need to take an conversation with the person in cause before make any action to complete our target satisfation, which is a good way to improve our social techniques and social experience, find more interesting people and more Love, that will give us enough knowledge to be a better person. ( Social Characteristics are related ( merit, courage, honor, assertive, etc... ))
